### **Star Schema vs Snowflake Schema in Data Warehouses**

A star schema contains a central fact table joined to denormalized dimension tables, optimizing for fast read queries with minimal joins. A snowflake schema normalizes dimension tables into secondary tables, reducing data redundancy at the cost of query performance due to extra joins.
