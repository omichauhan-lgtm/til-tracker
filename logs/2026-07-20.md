## Category: Distributed Systems
### **CAP Theorem: PACELC Extension**

While CAP states a system can only guarantee 2 of Consistency, Availability, or Partition Tolerance, PACELC extends this: If there is a Partition (P), trade off Availability (A) or Consistency (C); Else (E), trade off Latency (L) or Consistency (C) (e.g., MongoDB yields consistency for latency under normal operation).
