### **Consistent Hashing in Distributed Systems**

Consistent hashing minimizes key redistribution when scaling node counts. By mapping both keys and nodes to a circular space (the hash ring), adding or removing a node only impacts k/n keys. Virtual nodes are utilized to distribute load evenly across heterogeneous physical servers.
